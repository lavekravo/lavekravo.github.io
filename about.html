Lavekravo (nom de plume) is an artist and an educator who enjoys traditional as well as modern art styles. Their recent endeavors in the space of generative art explore the influence of carefully injected chaos in simple repetitive structures. Lavekravo's generative art works are at the confluence of minimalistic design aesthetics and swarming design patterns.

For collaborations, custom requests or commissions, exhibitions or general inquiries please feel free to reach out.
